About Crime Against Women

Crime against women is a serious problem that affects millions of women around the world. It can take many forms, including physical violence, sexual violence, emotional abuse, and economic abuse.

There are many laws in place to protect women from crime. However, these laws are not always enforced effectively. And even when they are enforced, they may not be enough to deter criminals or to provide adequate support for victims.

This website is dedicated to providing information about crime against women and the laws that protect women from crime. We hope that this information will help women to stay safe and to know their rights.

Laws for the Protection of Women in India

There are a number of laws in place to protect women in India. Some of the most important laws include:

  • The Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act, 2005
  • The Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013
  • The Criminal Law (Amendment) Act, 2013
  • The Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act, 2012
  • The Dowry Prohibition Act, 1961

These laws provide protection to women from a variety of crimes, including domestic violence, sexual harassment, sexual assault, and dowry-related violence.

If you are a woman who has been a victim of crime, please know that you are not alone. There are laws in place to protect you, and there are people who can help you. Please reach out to a trusted friend or family member, or contact a women's support organization for assistance.
